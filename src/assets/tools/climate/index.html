Definitions

Seasonal Rainfall is the total amount of rainfall, measured in mm, from 1st April to 31st November

Start of Rains is the first day after 1st April that gets 20mm of rainfall or more totalled over two consecutive days, but with no dry spell of 10 days or more in the next 21 days

End of Rains is the first day after 1st October with a water balance of 0

Season Length is the total number of days from the start of rains to the end of rains
